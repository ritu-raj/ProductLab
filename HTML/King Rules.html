#1 Build & Keep Building

What makes a man happy? It is when he builds & keeps building - be it his physique, assets, network

#2 Have a good EQ

Your thoughts' wavelength needs to match your partner's wavelength. The more the match, the merrier the relationship is!

#3 Choose wisely. Aim for long-term

Choose someone whose domain of work matches yours & whose thought-process ("mental wavelength" & beliefs) matches yours. Aim for a long-term relationship to have compounded growth & mental peace & bliss.

#4 Don't pry on others

When you have a relationship going on, don't allow your hormones to look outside your relationship. That's going to earn you bad karma!

#5 Be grateful. Keep pampering her.

Keep pampering her with small gifts from time to time. When she is grumpy, you can always go ahead & order her favorite ice-cream from her favorite ice-cream store

Some more rules...

  • Try to evolve together in your relationship with your partner
  • Maintain boundaries. At times, learn to say no
  • Always be a keen observer of what your partner says & does
  • Communicate effectively. Don't suppress your thoughts especially when it is important to express them
  • Be good. Trust your partner & have faith. Believe in Karma! What goes around comes around!
